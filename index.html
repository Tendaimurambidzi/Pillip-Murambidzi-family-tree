Our Family Story

The Murambidzi family legacy began with Simon Murambidzi and Maggie Dahwa, whose union laid the foundation for generations to come. Their son, Phillip Murambidzi (Senior), born on July 16, 1956, would become the family patriarch who, together with his beloved wife Sabina Marhanele (born March 15, 1967), built a beautiful family blessed with children, grandchildren, and a rich heritage of love and unity.

Today, the Murambidzi family continues to grow, with each new member adding to the tapestry of our shared history. This website serves as a living record of our family's journey - a place where memories are preserved, connections are celebrated, and the legacy of love and togetherness is passed down through generations.
