Family Story

Phillip Murambidzi (Senior) was born on 16 July 1956 to Simon Murambidzi and Maggie Dahwa, both now deceased. Phillip married Sabina Marhanele, born on 15 March 1967. Together, they built a family blessed with children, grandchildren, memories, and a growing legacy.

This website is a living family record. More names, spouses, children, photographs, stories, and special memories can be added as family continues to grow.
